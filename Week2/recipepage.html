Tortilla Egg Fiesta

By: Michael Nelson

Ingredients (serves only you):

  • 2 eggs (large brown preferred)
  • 1 tortilla (two if feeling frisky)
  • 2-3 sausages
  • butter
  • shredded jack cheddar cheese
  • 3/4 cup of milk (whole preferred)
  • salt grinder
  • pepper grinder
  • paprika
  • Trader Joe's Chili Pepper Sauce

Instructions (in excruciating detail):

  1. get a pan
  2. place on burner
  3. butter the pan
  4. place sausages on pan
  5. roll sausages around with tortilla (this will ensure a crisp tortilla!)
  6. get a bowl
  7. crack eggs in bowl
  8. whisk eggs around for a couple seconds
  9. pour milk
  10. add salt, pepper, and paprika
  11. whisk until eggs are fluidy
  12. add Trader Joe's Chili Pepper Sauce into mixture
  13. remove sausages from pan and place on plate
  14. pour bowl contents onto pan
  15. take in the aroma
  16. scramble the eggs
  17. either leave them in for delicious burnt eggs or plate them
  18. OPTIONAL: turn off smoke detector
  19. place eggs onto tortilla
  20. place sausage onto tortilla
  21. sprinkle jack cheddar on EVERYTHING
  22. pour even more Trader Joe's Chili Pepper Sauce on tortilla
  23. Bon Appetit!
